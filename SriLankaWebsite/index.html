Sri Lanka
Sri Lanka, formerly Ceylon, island country
lying in the Indian Ocean and separated
from peninsular India by the Palk Strait. It
is located between latitudes 5°55′ and
9°51′ N and longitudes 79°41′ and 81°53′ E
and has a maximum length of 268 miles
(432 km) and a maximum width of 139
miles (224 km).
